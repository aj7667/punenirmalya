Privacy Policy

Pune Nirmalya Finder

Effective date: 23-06-2026  ·  Last updated: 23-06-2026

Pune Nirmalya Finder (“the app”, “we”, “us”) is a free, non-commercial civic service operated by ILXS that helps people in Pune find nearby Nirmalya Kalash and collection points to dispose of floral and puja (nirmalya) waste responsibly. This policy explains what information the app handles and how.

We do not sell your data, we do not show advertising, and we do not require you to create an account or share your name, phone number, or email to use the app.

1. Information we handle

  • Approximate location (optional). If you tap “Near me” or choose “Use my location”, the app reads your device’s location to show the nearest points or to help you tag a location you are suggesting. This happens in your browser. We do not store your personal location on our servers — unless you choose to submit it as the coordinates of a collection point (see below).
  • Information you submit. When you use “Suggest a location”, the place name, area, type, accepted items, and map coordinates you enter are saved to our database and shown publicly to other users. When you report a status (Available / Full / Closed), that status is saved anonymously.
  • Administrator accounts. A small number of moderators sign in with an email and password (managed by Supabase) to verify or remove points. General users do not have accounts.
  • Technical logs. Our hosting and database providers may automatically record limited technical information (such as IP address, browser type, and time of access) for security, reliability, and abuse prevention.

2. How we use information

  • To show you nearby collection points and directions.
  • To display community-submitted locations and live status to all users.
  • To maintain the security, integrity, and accuracy of the map data (including moderating submissions).

3. Location permission is optional

You can use the app without granting location access — you can still browse, search, and view all points. If you previously allowed location and want to stop, you can revoke the permission anytime in your browser or device settings.

4. Public content

Locations you suggest and status reports you submit are visible to everyone using the app. Please do not include personal or sensitive information in these submissions.

5. Third-party services

The app relies on the following services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • GitHub Pages — website hosting.
  • Supabase — database, realtime sync, and administrator authentication.
  • OpenStreetMap — map tiles.
  • Google Maps — opens only when you tap “Get directions”, after which Google’s own policy applies.

6. Data retention & your choices

Submitted locations and status reports are kept for as long as they remain useful to the service. If you would like a submission you made corrected or removed, contact us at the address below and we will act on reasonable requests, subject to applicable law (including India’s Digital Personal Data Protection Act, where relevant).

7. Children

The app is intended for general public use and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect the data we handle. However, no method of transmission or storage over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above.

10. Contact

For any questions or requests regarding this policy, contact us at projectclean371@gmail.com.
